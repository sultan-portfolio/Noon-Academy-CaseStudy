North Star: Learning Gain per Effort
The North Star

Not all effort is equal.

The ultimate measure of student success in this dataset is whether effort converts to mastery. My North Star Metric is Learning Gain per Effort (LGpE), ensuring every click in Noon translates into real knowledge. Once I found this metric, every piece of analysis had to connect back to it. If a data point doesn't explain why LGpE is high or low, it gets dropped.

LGpE = (EOC − Diagnostic) / Effort
where Effort = Attempts% + Missions% (all values normalized)
  • Prevents busywork from looking like progress
  • Highlights hidden churn risk (high effort, low ROI)
  • Gives ops a clear intervention trigger
Diagnosis
What's Happening

Learning gains exist, but outcomes vary widely because the system doesn't reliably convert effort into mastery, and classroom execution varies.

Why It's Happening

3 recurring patterns explain most of the variance: Misguided Effort, High Potential / Low Structure Fit, and the Teacher Paradox.

What We Do Tomorrow

Build Detection + Adaptation loops that are simple, scalable, and move learning gains fast.

Risk Mitigation

Bold actions require sharp checks. My strategy assumes that data is the map, but the classroom is the terrain. We don't just implement — we validate and pivot.

01 // Validation
Data Validation Phase
Before launching any automated trigger, validate the noise. Attempt Integrity: filter for genuine cognitive effort before flagging. Level Normalization: a 25% accuracy in Level 1 differs from Level 2 — triggers must be dynamic, not static. Metric Validity: correlate Trust with LTV before using it as a success proxy.
02 // Operations
Operational Assumptions
School B is the Golden Standard. It started with the weakest diagnostic (0.43) and achieved the highest learning gain (0.217) — not who ends highest, but who grows most from where they started. Tech stack must support real-time event triggers. Facilitators must have pedagogical training to act on visibility layer data.
03 // Integrity
Goodhart's Law Defense
Every system designed to be measured is designed to be gamed. Cobra Effect: prevent grade inflation via independent spot-checks. Efficiency Trap: pair LGpE with a Challenge Index — efficient mastery of hard concepts, not fast completion of easy ones. Human Friction: frame interventions as "Support Sprints," never punitive.
04 // Segmentation
Segmentation Integrity
Onboarding questions are a starting point only. Within 7 days, the system overwrites classification based on behavioral data. We trust what the student does in the app more than what they say in a survey.
05 // Scalability
1:1 Sessions as Discovery
1:1 escalations are a Day-1 Discovery Tool, not a long-term scaling strategy. Qualitative data from these sessions feeds into an Automated Adaptive Engine for 100x scalability. Use them to understand why students fail, then remove the human bottleneck.
06 // Benchmark
The School B Standard
School B started with the weakest baseline but produced the highest learning gain. This is the real story in the data. Not who ended highest, but who transformed the most. School B is the benchmark every other school should be audited against.
